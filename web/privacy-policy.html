Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Draconia Quiz ("the App") is a personality quiz app that helps you discover which dragon type and element you are. This policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. The App does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email address, or any other identifying information, and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Local Storage

The App stores your preferences (language selection, audio volume) locally on your device using Android's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and can be removed at any time by uninstalling the App.

Analytics & Tracking

The App contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking pixels, and no third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Internet Access

The App does not require an internet connection to function. No data is sent to or received from any server during normal use.

Children

The App is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no data, no special provisions for children are required.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the developer via the GitHub repository.
